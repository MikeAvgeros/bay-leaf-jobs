Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How can I apply to jobs?

To apply to a job, you need to register and sign in to you account, then click the button 'View all jobs'. Find your favourite job, click apply and fill out the required form.

Q. How can I withdraw my application?

You are unable to withdraw your job application on this website. If you are no longer interested, please contact the recruiter directly on the email provided.

Q. Shall I contact the recruiter directly?

We highly recommend to not contact the recruiter directly, but rather use the application form on the website. For any changes to your status or application, or if it has been more than 2 weeks since your application date then you can contact the recruiter directly.

Q. How can I create a job?

You can only create a job if you register as a recruiter. Once signed in to your account, simply click the button 'Create a job' and fill out the required form.

Q. Where can I find the jobs I have applied to?

You can view all the jobs you have applied to on your personal profile page. Sign in to your account and click 'Profile'. All the jobs will be under 'Job I applied to'.

Q. Where can I find the applicants for the jobs I created?

To view all the applicants for the jobs you have created, you need to view the full job description and click on the 'Applicants' button. To open the full job description, click on the relevant job card in your profile under 'Jobs I created'.

Q. Where can I find the jobs I have created?

You can view all the jobs you have created to on your personal profile page. Sign in to your account and click 'Profile'.

Q. How can I edit / delete a job I've created?

You can view all the jobs you have created to on your personal profile page. Sign in to your account and click 'Profile'. Then simply click on the job you wish to update or delete and click the 'Update' or 'Delete' buttons.

Q. Can I edit /cancel my profile?

Yes you can update or delete your profile by going to your profile page. The update and delete buttons will be at the bottom of your profile card on the left side of the page.
