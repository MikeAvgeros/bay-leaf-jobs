Terms of Service

We take note of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR); however, it’s business as usual here at bayleafjobs.


To explain: We have been obsessive about privacy since we launched the site. That’s why we don’t have a CV database, and we don’t ask you for masses of personal information when you register (just a username, email address location – and a password, which we encrypt).


We never advertise the site, and we never buy email lists – so all our users found us themselves and elected to sign up. We don’t work with any social media, and we don’t disclose any details about any of our users to anyone, ever. We never send promotional mailings, newsletters or any other correspondence to our users. Of course if you apply for a job, your details will go to the advertiser, and copies of your cover letter and CV remain on our system for your own and the recruiter's use.
